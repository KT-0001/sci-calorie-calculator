SCI Calorie Calculator

Brief

This calculator is designed for spinal cord injury nutrition planning using assumptions informed by published SCI energy expenditure and macro guidance. Generic calorie calculators are built for non-SCI populations and often do not work well here, so this tool applies SCI-specific adjustment factors.

Method

How each part of the calculator maps to the literature above:
  • REE model selection: generic Mifflin-St Jeor or SCI-adjusted pathway based on SCI REE validation studies [1,2,3,4].
  • SCI adjustment factor (level and AIS): approximates lower REE in SCI populations and can be applied in the SCI model path [1,2,5].
  • Phase and pressure injury context: adds clinical-context calorie/protein adjustments, especially during rehabilitation and wound-healing risk periods [4,7].
  • Goal deltas and macro structure: informed by SCI nutrition/dietetics evidence and practice-focused reviews [6,8].
  • Output is a starting estimate; weekly body-weight and composition trend should guide refinement.
